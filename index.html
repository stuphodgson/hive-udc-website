What is this all about?

All employers owe you a duty of care to prevent damage to your hearing whilst at work. If they have breached that duty then you are entitled to seek compensation

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make a claim?

If you have worked in a noisy environment at some point since 1963 and you suffer from damage to your hearing you may be able to make a claim for compensation.

What if my claim is successful?

If your claim is successful we will recover compensation for the damage caused to your hearing. We can also make a claim for hearing aids or treatment for your condition.

How much does it Cost?
